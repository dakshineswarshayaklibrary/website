About Us

Dakshineswar Shayak Library was founded in 8th September,1996 and till today, it is helping undergraduate students by providing renowned textbooks of many subjects. It is a collaborative library which is managed by the students and professionals of the locality. Our library has a precious collection of thousands of valuable & rare books with a view to provide the members unbeatable level of knowledge & proper guidance in numerous fields & subjects.
